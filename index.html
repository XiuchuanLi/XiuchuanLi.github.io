Biography

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at the School of Computer Science, The University of Sydney (USYD). I received the master's degree from Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Science in 2023. I received the bachelor's degree from School of Automation Science and Electrical Engineering, Beihang University in 2020. My research focuses on causal discovery and trustworthy machine learning.
